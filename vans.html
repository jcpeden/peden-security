Van Security

Van burglary is at an all-time high in Ireland and it has never been more important to have your van secured than now.

We have been designing and fitting security locks to vans and commercial vehicles for over twenty years. As such, we’re best-placed to advise on single-owner or business-fleet applications.

We can explain the best lock solution for your specific security requirements.

What We Offer

  • Mobile fitting, on-site
  • Slamlocks
  • Deadlocks
  • High security locks
  • Armoured handles
  • Loom guards
  • Handle shields
  • Truck and trailer locks
